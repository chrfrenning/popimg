How to use the Automated Photo Booth

The Automated Photo Booth is our most loved feature - and you now have access!

For best results, follow these simple steps:

  • Setup a dedicated space on your event for group photos
  • A neutral background is best, or use a professional backdrop if you can
  • Make sure the lighting is good, using a professional ring light is a good idea
  • Use a ring light with a smartphone holder, or get a camera stand with a smartphone holder
  • Use a smartphone with a high quality camera and use landscape mode
  • Open the LiveWall Photo Booth App on the smartphone and get going
  • If you don't want AI to snap for you, get a selfie-stick remote, connect it to your smartphone, and use it to take photos
